When was the last time India used nine bowling options? Well it was their first ever in World Cup history. Making the most of their inconsequential league game against Netherlands on Sunday at the M Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru, India did a bit of a bowling experiment as they tested their part-time options which included Virat Kohli and captain Rohit Sharma himself. The skipper later revealed that it was all according to their pre-match plan of testing their options for a sixth bowler as India
